## Design Context

### Users
Perth homeowners, builders, shopfitters, commercial operators, and facilities managers comparing polished concrete, honed concrete, grind-and-seal, and epoxy flooring. They need confidence that Perth Concrete Care can assess the slab, recommend the right system, and deliver a durable finish with minimal friction.

### Brand Personality
Precise, premium, practical. The site should feel like an architectural specification tool with trade credibility: restrained, sharp, confident, and image-led rather than salesy.

### Aesthetic Direction
Refined editorial minimalism inspired by high-end service finder and architectural portfolio layouts. Use Plus Jakarta Sans as the core body voice, quiet warm neutrals, black/charcoal contrast, and PCC mint green (#bfffbd) as a precise signal accent. Real flooring imagery should carry the brand; avoid orange accents, decorative gradients, generic cards, oversized marketing fluff, and noisy boxed layouts.

### Design Principles
- Lead with real surfaces and project evidence instead of decorative UI.
- Keep the grid disciplined: strong alignment, generous margins, small type where appropriate, and clear left-to-right scanning.
- Use mint green sparingly for wayfinding, focus, and decision points.
- Prefer flat editorial bands, dividers, and whitespace over nested cards or heavy shadows.
- Make quote/contact paths obvious without making every action loud.
